Fun Facts about Pizza in the Unisted States!

  1. Tips for Cooking Pizzas in a Wood-burning brick oven Pizza
    • Start with the pizza further away from the fire
    • Wait for the pizza to cook to a more solid form before attempting to move it again
    • Rotate the pizza when the side closest to the fire turns the desired darkness
    • Check the bottom of the pizza and move it closer to the fire until it reaches the desired darkness
    • If needed, raise the pizza up toward the top of the oven for a few seconds to crisp up the crust
